Overview

A surgical instrument is a tool or equipment that has been specifically created for carrying out a certain activity or producing desired effects during an operation or surgery, such as changing biological tissue. Various categories of surgical instruments exist. The instruments used in surgical operation include cutting instruments, grasping and holding instruments, haemostatic forceps (instruments used to halt blood), retractors, clamps and distracters, accessories, and implants.

Basic Instrument Used in Operation Theater

A surgical instrument is a tool or device that has been specifically created for carrying out a specific action or producing desired effects during an operation or surgery, such as modifying biological tissue. Various categories of surgical instruments exist.

  • Cutting equipment
  • Holding and grabbing objects
  • Forceps for hemostasis ( instruments used to stop blood)
  • Retractors
  • Shackles and eye-catchers
  • Adornments and implants
